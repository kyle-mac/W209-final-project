A Brief History

In 2008 an unknown author releases a paper (Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System) under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto that highlights the potential of a cryptocurrency called Bitcoin to be revolutionize modern payment systems in . A year later Bitcoin mining begins and the landscape of cryptocurrencies begins to expand.

Rise and Fall

The cryptocurrency market has seen continued expansion and evolution since Bitcoins initial creation in 2009. While early years showed slow growth and expansion, the popularity of Bitcoin and other currencies exploded in 2017 before crashing shortly after 2018. With this tool we hope to examine the regulations that lead to these trends as well as further explore the growth of these currencies on a global scale.
